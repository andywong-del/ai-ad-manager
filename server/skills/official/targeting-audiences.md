# Targeting & Audiences

## When to Use
When user asks about audiences, targeting strategy, or wants to build/improve audiences.

## Audience Strategy

### Retargeting (warm audiences)
- **Video viewers** — people who watched your videos (75%+ = high intent)
- **Website visitors** — pixel-based, URL rules for specific pages
- **Page/IG engagement** — people who interacted with your content
- **Lead form openers** — opened but didn't submit

### Prospecting (cold audiences)
- **Lookalike** — find people similar to your best customers (1% = most similar, 10% = broadest)
- **Interest targeting** — target by interests, behaviors, demographics
- **Broad/Advantage+** — let Meta's algorithm find your audience

### Best Practices
- Start with 1% lookalike from purchasers/converters
- Layer retargeting with 7-day, 30-day, 90-day windows
- Exclude converters from prospecting to avoid wasted spend
- Check audience overlap — audiences competing against each other raise costs

## Audience Selection Guide

| Goal | Recommended audience | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Scale sales | Lookalike 1-3% from purchasers | Proven buyer profile |
| Reduce CPA | Retarget video viewers 75% | High intent, low cost |
| Brand awareness | Broad or interest-based | Maximum reach |
| Re-engage | Website visitors 30 days | Already know you |

## Overlap Detection

When multiple audiences are active:
- Flag audiences with > 20% overlap
- Recommend exclusions or consolidation
- Suggest combining small audiences

## Retention Windows

| Audience type | Default | Range |
|---|---|---|
| Video viewers | 30 days | 1-365 days |
| Website visitors | 30 days | 1-180 days |
| Engagement | 30 days | 1-365 days |
| Customer list | N/A | Until removed |

## Output Format

1. Current audience overview (types, sizes, overlap)
2. Recommendations based on user's goal
3. Specific audience to create with parameters
